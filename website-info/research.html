Research agenda

Research logic

Features build views. Views build structures. Structures build maps. Maps reveal change.

F

Features

Attributes of datasets, entities, regions, projects or markets.

V

Views

Datasets and evidence layers: on-chain, market, developer, infrastructure, remote sensing.

L

Localisation

Products, projects, assets or regions represented inside bounded neighbourhoods.

G

Global map

Combined local structures form a taxonomy-level or system-level representation.

T

Temporal change

Repeated global maps reveal drift, instability, structural breakdown and anomaly signals.
